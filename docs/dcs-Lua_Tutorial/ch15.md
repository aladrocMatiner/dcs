## How it is used in DCS

The idea of recipes:

- You keep small, reliable building blocks.
- You reuse them across missions.

This chapter includes recipes for:

- `say(text)` (message)
- `log(text)` (log)
- `getFlag(n)` (read flag)
- `scheduleEvery(n, fn)` (safe repeating)
